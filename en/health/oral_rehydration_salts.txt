Oral Rehydration Salts (ORS)
----------------------------
Purpose: prevent death from dehydration (diarrhea, cholera, heat).

Ingredients (per 1 liter clean water):
- 6 level teaspoons sugar
- 0.5 level teaspoon salt

Instructions:
- Mix well until dissolved.
- Give small sips frequently.
- Store no longer than 24 hours.

Note:
Too much sugar -> worse diarrhea.
Too much salt -> dangerous.

Safe, simple, lifesaving.
